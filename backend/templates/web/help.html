Help & Documentation

  • Use the top navigation to access Groups, Posts, Reports, and Profile.
  • Groups: create, edit, and delete groups you own. Click a group to view its posts.
  • Posts: upload an image to a group with a caption. Bulk‑delete your posts from the list.
  • Import/Export: export your posts to CSV, or import simple CSVs to create placeholder posts.
  • Reports: view a simple chart of your posts over the last 7 days.
  • API: JSON endpoints are available under /api/ for integration.
